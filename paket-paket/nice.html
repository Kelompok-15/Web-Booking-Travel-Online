        Nice
  
        Nice, the fifth largest city in France, acts as a magnet attracting 
        people from all over the world, for a multitude of reasons, Not 
        only renowned for its grace, Nice has become a hub for research in 
        industry, science and advanced technology since the creation of such 
        centers as Acropolis and Sophia Antipolis. The Palais de Congres, a 
        convention center, can accomodate diverse functions.

        Both elegant and simple, Nice's charm comes from its exceptional 
        climate as well as its beauty. Even in the winter the cafe terraces 
        are filled with people happily drinking and contemplating the ultra 
        famous Promenade des Anglais. Always in flower this wonderful walk 
        follows along the bay, looking out onto the Baie des Anges and the 
        beaches on one side, and on the other such luxury hotels as the 
        Negresco.
